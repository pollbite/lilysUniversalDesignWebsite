Fu Xuan's Kit

Fu Xuan is nigh unkillable, and due to her ability to deflect the damage that the team takes to her, it makes the entire team unkillable. She has so many tools in her kit that practically make her an immovable wall it's insane. She is extremely good at what she doesâ€¦ which being tanking damage and keeping your team alive indirectly she is a great option as a defensive unit and easily will be able to solo sustain your team!

Her only con is that she relies upon her ultimate in order to provide team and personal heals. Considerate timing in battle, especially battles with enemies that are aggressive and deal high AOE damage, is required. Additionally, keep in mind her energy recharge when building her relics to ensure that she can reliably and consistently ult and keep her stacks of healing up.

Fu Xuan's Lightcone

She Already Shut Her Eyes

Fu Xuan's signature lightcone is called "She Already Shut her Eyes". This lightcone is incredibly overpowered. It heals the entire team and buffs damage percent. This lightcone takes Fu Xuan from simply a preservation unit to a multi-role harmony and abundance unit. Of course, as with all signature lightcones, this is a premium option and you must contend with the fickle nature of the lightcone banner.

Texture of Memories

Another great five star lightcone for Fu Xuan is "Texture of Memories", which is free in the Simulated Universe Herta Shop. This the best lightcone for making Fu Xuan unkillable. This also gives Effect RES, which is important for the Keel relic set.

Moment of Victory

This is Gepard's signature lightcone that can be obtained through the standard banner. This is a premium option for Fu Xuan, and a good generalist five star lightcone for most preservation units.

Landau's Choice

This is Fu Xuan's best 4 star lightcone option. This lightcone gives more damage reduction, which means more sustain for Fu Xuan. This also increases taunt on her.

Anything Else

Fu Xuan is versatile, so she's not reliant on any one lightcone to work. Therefore, you can use any preservation lightcone effectively if needed.

Fuxuan's Relics

Guard

A 4pc guard relic set gives both energy and healing when her HP is under 50%. This is the best set for a pure survival build.

Longevous

A 4pc Longevous set will be Fu Xuan's best mix between damage and sustain.

Genius

A 4pc Genius set would be her best damage option, but isn't reccomended for her sustain builds. This is a relatively niche build.

2pc Combos

2pc combos of Guard, Longevous, and Knight are also great optoins for sustain, and are more efficient to farm.

Keel

This is in overpowered set on her, and gives 10% crit damage to all party members.

Fleet

Gives 8% ATK% to all party members. This is a fairly good set, but Keel is better.

Fuxuan's Synergies

Fu Xuan is incredibly flexible; she can find her place in any team that needs a sustain unit. She works especially well with units that require being damaged for their kit, like Blade, as her sustain comes mostly through her damage reduction. She is especially touted for her ability to function in mono quantum teams.

Fuxuan's Talent Priority

Level her talent first, then her ultimate, and then her basic attack (if you level it at all). Fu xuan makes good use out of all her talents so I'd recommend leveling them all (excluding her basic attack).
