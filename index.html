About Me

Hi! My name is Lily. I'm a junior in Creative Technology and Design, with a minor in Art Practices. My current focus is on graphic design. I work as a design associate with the ATLAS Institute administration, and I also work the front desk in the administrative office.

In my free time, I love playing video games, playing Dungeons and Dragons, and drawing. My favorite game is Genshin Impact, but I also play Honkai Star Rail, Reverse 1999 and Twisted Wonderland. I'm a freelance digital artist, and I often draw fan art for my favorite series!
