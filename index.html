About Me

I work as a design associate with the ATLAS Institute administration, and I also work the front desk in the administrative office (feel free to drop by and say hi). In my free time, I love playing video games, playing Dungeons and Dragons, and drawing. I'm a freelance digital artist, and I often draw fan art for my favorite series!
